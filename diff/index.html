gwei-names — contract diff

Every line that differs between the ownerless gwei-names contracts and the upstream wei-names they fork (z0r0z/wei-names@bb47188). Generated with diff -u; nothing hidden.

What actually changed

  • No owner, no admin. Removed Ownable (import, inheritance, and the constructor's _initializeOwner(tx.origin)) and every owner-only function: setDefaultFee, setLengthFees, clearLengthFee, setPremiumSettings, and withdraw. There is no privileged role left.
  • Fees fixed & burned. The mutable fee storage (defaultFee, lengthFees, maxPremium, premiumDecayPeriod) becomes constants (FEE_LEN1…4, DEFAULT_FEE, MAX_PREMIUM, PREMIUM_DECAY_PERIOD) and getFee goes view → pure. With no withdraw and no owner, paid ETH is locked in the contract forever — effectively burned.
  • Admin plumbing removed. The admin events (DefaultFeeChanged, LengthFeeChanged, …) and admin-only errors (LengthMismatch, PremiumTooHigh, DecayPeriodTooLong) are gone; Unauthorized is now declared directly (it used to come from Ownable).
  • .wei → .gwei. TLD namehash WEI_NODE → GWEI_NODE (new value 0xcca9…7d7f), name()/symbol() → "Gwei Name Service"/"GWEI", and the suffix strings + the suffix-stripping logic (4-byte .wei → 5-byte .gwei).
  • SubdomainRegistrar. The hardcoded mainnet name address constant becomes immutable, injected via the constructor — gwei deploys a fresh NameNFT instead of pointing at a known mainnet address.
